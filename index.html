What is a Bubbi?

Bubbi: the Yiddish word for grandmother of Jewish or Eastern European origin

You know the type: an endless supply of floral housecoats, hot in the winter and cold in the summer, careful with her money — but generous with kisses and a pinch on the cheek (from BeyondBubbi.com). This modern Bubbi is full of chocolate chips and sprinkles.

Bubbi’s Boys

With help from Bubbi’s Boys — trusty sous-chefs (also known as grandsons) — Bubbi bakes almost daily, turning out cookies, cakes, and old-fashioned favorites. We’d love to help with your next sweet craving.
