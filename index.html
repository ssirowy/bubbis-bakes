What is a Bubbi?

Bubbi: the Yiddish word for Grandmother of Jewish or Eastern European origin

You know the type, an endless supply of floral print housecoats, hot in the winter and cold in the summer, careful with her money, but generous with kisses and pinches of the cheek” (from BeyondBubbi.com). But this modern Bubbi is full of chocolate chips and sprinkles!

Bubbi's Boys

With the help of Bubbi’s Boys, trusty sous chefs also known as grandsons, Bubbi bakes almost daily putting out dozens of cookies, cakes and other old-fashioned treats. We’d love to help you with your sweet cravings.
